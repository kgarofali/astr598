>>qsub -I -l walltime=3:00:00 (start interactive session on hyak, length 3 hours)
>>module avail (software avail on hyak)
>>module (un)load <module_name> (to choose/unchoose version of python, for example)
>>module list (list all software you've loaded, i.e. added to bashrc)
